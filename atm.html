ATM Application

Project in SPD Milan - Digital Typography

Web design Program

ATM is a User Experience project, its objectif to design and prototype a web application to purchase ATM tickets online. During the first phase, we worked on the Kiosk interface (metro stations), analysed it and tried to fix the user gaps and problems. In the second one we worked on the ATM WebApp by defining differents tasks:

Research, Observations and Interviews.
