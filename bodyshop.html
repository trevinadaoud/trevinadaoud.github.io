The Body Shop

New Campaign

Project in SPD Milan - Integrated Communication

Web and Visual design Program

Group project by Elisabetta Cona - Greg Palmieri - Jan Riha and Trevina Daoud

Objective

There is a new The Body Shop out there. It has thousands of products and a brand new commerce platform. Tell a new story!! What kind of campaign The Body Shop is going to have?? State the touchpoints: social media, store, site… and their roles in the campaign.

Our Concept

Our Idea is to promote eco-friendly Body Shop lifestyle, art, cultures connected to ingredients and build an awareness about environmental risks for children's health. The concept is to take the 14 key ingredients of the bodyshop and assign each one of them to a land artist who will create a special piece of art with it.

Our campaign will follow 14 various artists, each one of them will be guided by a person from The Body Shop of the country during their journey. During their stay, they will discover different cultures connected to trade communities. All the artists will spend one month in the country selected taking a digital diary of their experience, that people will follow on social media.After discovering their lifestyle and culture, the artist will be inspired to create the piece of art related to the place with the helping of the community children.

This campaign will involve children and support their health problems, caused by environmental risks, with a part of the money gain from the ingredient products sold in stores.

It focuses on the word “SEED” and its relation with the key points of the campaign:
1- Nature/ingredients
2- Art
3- Children .

The Seed of Art

Nature: seeds of the ingredients themselves.
Art: as a seed that will let the land art more known - seeding beauty back.
Children: seeds of the future, of the new society

Ecosystem

- Banner in the actual e-commerce.
- Dedicated Microsite.
- Mainly social media: Instagram, Facebook, Twitter.
- Hashtags: #TheSeedOfArt #LandArtExperience #SeedingBeautyBack #KidsSeedsOfFuture
- Magazines: Fashion magazines / Travel magazines: National Geographics and Vanity Fair.
- Montage regrouping the whole videos. A Teaser for the second event that will be organised with a different Artist.
- Store: Packaging, Section for a Book that groups all the artists' journeys and their piece of art

Richard Shilling - ARTFUL JOURNEY IN CHINA

Richard Shilling is an artist from UK. He works mostly as a land artist and sculptor.
Let’s take him to China to explore the Chinese culture and help him creating a wonderful promotion for our brand and products. Our plan is to give Schilling as much artistic freedom as he needs, but at the same time he should be inspired by the Chinese culture in every way as the art he is going to create at while exploring China.

Goal of artist’s journey is to promote the Camellia flower as an ingredient from China and involve children in his activities. This will be done mostly by digital diary in form of a blog and Instagram channel.

Richard Shilling will be working with chinese kids, in a form of a workshop. The main part of this campaign: the community kids, children will work with the artist to have a final piece of art. Part of the money will be used to support their health caused by environmental risks.
